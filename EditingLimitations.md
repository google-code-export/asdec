# Editing limitations #

## ActionScript 1/2 ##
Editing of ActionScript is limited to modifications of existing P-code (assembler).
You can't create new scripts or add new scripts to existing sprites.

## ActionScript 3 ##
Editing of ActionScript is limited to modifications of existing P-code (assembler). You can modify method parameters, class atributes type and default values.
You can't create new classes, methods or attributes. You can't create new multinames, namespaces or namespace sets in constant table.
